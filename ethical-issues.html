Ethical Considerations

Effect on healthcare: 
In Australia from 2014 to 2015 63.4% of adults were overweight or obese meaning an overwhelming number of Australia’s will apply for gastric banding.

Gastric banding is extremely costly and therefore healthcare cannot fund the whole bill. Patients will usually need to pay $3,000 to $10,000 US dollars depending on if they have healthcare or not.
