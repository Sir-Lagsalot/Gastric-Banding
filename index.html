Intro & History

​Introduction
The gastric band (otherwise known as the lap band) is an adjustable band made of silicone, that is wrapped around the top part of the stomach. This limits food intake and increases digestion time, and is therefore used for patients that are morbidly obese (i.e. with a BMI of above 35).
​
This technique is extremely effective at losing weight, with patients losing up to 500g to 1kg of weight every day. However, this is an extreme measure, and should only be used when there is no alternative. The solution is not permanent, as gastric bands only last for 9 months.
